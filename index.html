Welcome

What to Expect on This Site

This is a personal study scrapbook for number theory — built to document my mathematical journey from the foundations of integers all the way through advanced topics like elliptic curves and Fermat's Last Theorem. Here's how it's organized:

📖 Learn
Step through units in order. Each topic takes you through the formal theory, a visualization, my personal intuition, a proof strategy, and practice problems. You can track your progress as you go.
🃏 Glossary
Flashcards for every key term. Each card reveals a formal definition, a plain-English translation, and a concrete numerical example — so you see the math from three angles at once.
✍️ Proofs
Handwritten and typeset proofs of major theorems, plus an honest look at my mistakes and how I corrected them. Math is learned by getting things wrong first.
🔍 Mysteries
A gallery of unsolved problems in number theory — conjectures that have resisted proof for centuries. These put the whole subject in perspective.

All content is authored by the site creator. Mathematical notation is rendered via KaTeX.
